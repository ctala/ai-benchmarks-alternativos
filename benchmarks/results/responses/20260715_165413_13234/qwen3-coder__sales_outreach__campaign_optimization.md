## **1. Cálculo de CTR, CPC, CPA y Conversion Rate**

### **Fórmulas clave:**

- **CTR (Click-Through Rate)** = (Clicks / Impresiones) × 100  
- **CPC (Cost Per Click)** = Costo / Clicks  
- **Conversion Rate (CR)** = (Signups / Clicks) × 100  
- **CPA (Cost Per Acquisition)** = Costo / Signups  

---
