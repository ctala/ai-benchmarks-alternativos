### 3. TENDENCIAS PRINCIPALES (observadas H2 2024 → momentum 2025)

| # | Tendencia | Evidencia / Detalle |
|---|-----------|---------------------|
| **1. Climate Tech = nueva vertical "fintech-size"** | Fondos dedicados: **Kairos (BR), Climatech (MX), Satgana (regional)**. BNDES, BID Invest, DFC capital catalítico. Solar distribuido, biofuels, carbon MRV, mining decarb. |
| **2. "Series A Crunch" selectivo pero no terminal** | Valuations 20-30% below 2021; **revenue multiples 8-12x ARR** (vs 25-40x). Requisitos: $1-2M ARR + net retention >100% + path to profitability 24-36m. |
| **3. Corporate VC + Family Offices >30% deals** | FEMSA, Grupo Bolívar, Credicorp, Itaú, BTG, Bancolombia, Arcor, Sigma — **lead o co-lead rounds**. Buscan strategic optionality + financial return. |
| **4. Secondary market se institucionaliza** | **Capshare, Equidam, Forge, AngelList** activando liquidez early. Fundadores/empleados venden 10-20% en Series B/C. Fondos "continuation vehicles" surgen (ej. **Kaszek Continuity, ONEVC Follow-on**). |
| **5. Nearshoring real → Logistics + Industrial Tech + Energy** | México (Bajío, Monterrey) + Colombia (Barranquilla, Cartagena) + Perú. Fondos: **Dila Capital, ALLVP, Wollef** thesis-driven. |
| **6. Talent arbitrage: LatAm → Global SaaS** | Startups **incorporan en DE/US, venden global, team 80% LatAm**. YC W24/S24: ~18% batch = LatAm founders (record). |
